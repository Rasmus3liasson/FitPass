## English

### Vision & ambition

FitPass is a modern fitness club aggregator built around freedom and flexibility.

Instead of being locked into a single gym or long term membership, users get one flexible membership that can change month to month. You decide what you want to train and where you want to train it.

FitPass is built for everyone. From the everyday exerciser who enjoys trying different types of workouts, to highly active users who like changing environments and not feeling tied to one specific club.

At the same time FitPass helps gyms and studios fill more class slots, reach new users and make better use of their capacity.

At its core FitPass is about freedom. For users and for clubs.

### Why I‚Äôm building FitPass

The fitness industry is fragmented. Users jump between apps, memberships and booking systems, while gyms struggle with visibility, engagement and empty class slots.

FitPass brings everything together in one place. Users can discover, compare and book workouts easily. Clubs get a modern platform without having to build or maintain their own systems.

### What makes FitPass different

- Multiple gyms and studios in one app
- Flexible membership that can change every month
- Users choose freely what and where they want to train
- Fits both casual exercisers and heavy trainers
- Focus on freedom and variety, not lock in
- Real time availability, reviews and recommendations
- Dedicated flows for users and club owners
- Helps clubs fill more classes and unused capacity
- Built with real world business needs and scalability in mind

### Core features

**For users**

- User accounts and profiles
- Club and class discovery with smart filtering
- Simple booking and flexible memberships
- Training history and activity stats
- Social features like friends, messaging and activity feeds
- Push notifications for reminders and updates

**For club owners**

- Dashboard for managing clubs, classes and schedules
- Booking and capacity management
- Insights and basic analytics
- Secure payments and subscriptions

### Tech stack

- Mobile app: React Native with Expo
- Web app: Next.js
- Backend: Node.js with Express
- Database: Supabase with PostgreSQL
- Shared TypeScript monorepo
- Monitoring with Sentry
- Styling with Tailwind CSS, NativeWind and custom design system
